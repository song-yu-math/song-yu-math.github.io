Song Yu  喻淞

Email: songyu at caltech dot edu

I am a joint postdoctoral fellow at California Institute of Technology and Tsinghua University Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, supervised by Nicolai Reshetikhin and Tony Yue Yu. For 2024-26, I am in residence at Tsinghua. I am broadly interested in algebraic geometry, enumerative geometry, and mirror symmetry.

I am a recent PhD graduate from Columbia University, advised by Chiu-Chu Melissa Liu. Before, I was an undergraduate student at Pomona College, advised by Erica Flapan and Shahriar Shahriari. I am also an alumnus of Budapest Semesters in Mathematics and Math in Moscow.
